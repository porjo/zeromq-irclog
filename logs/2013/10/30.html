[01:12]cremes beginnner: It depends on how your OS handles inter-process communication. On Windows, ipc is handled via sockets. On linux & osx, it uses sockets too but with a faster path through the stack.
[01:12]cremes and inproc is for communicating *within* the *same* process. You can't use inproc to communication between separate processes.
[01:12]cremes so in your case, the performance difference is moot.
[01:12]cremes If you language doesn't support threads, I doubt it's a speed demon anyway. Just use tcp or ipc.
